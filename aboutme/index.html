Professional Biography

Fred, professionally known as ItsRede, is a multi-disciplinary creator based in Columbus, Ohio. Born on August 21, 2005, Fred has cultivated a significant digital footprint through music production, literature, and community leadership.

Music & Production

Specializing in Breakcore and Instrumental Electronic music, ItsRede focuses on the synergy between audio and high-retention digital media. As the owner of Rede Records, an independent label, he manages the creative direction and distribution for a growing catalog of original works. His production style is characterized by intricate rhythms and precise "punchy" audio engineering.

Literary Career

In 2026, ItsRede expanded into the literary world, publishing works that analyze digital trends and online culture. His titles include "Shorts To Success" and "How To Not Be A Larper Online," both available on Google Play Books.

Digital Advocacy & Communities

Beyond creative media, ItsRede is the founder of Terming Gods X, a digital safety and advocacy collective active on the Roblox platform. Through this organization and his development work in Luau scripting, he focuses on community security and analytics-driven game management.
